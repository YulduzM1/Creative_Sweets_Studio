Fresh Cookies

Ingredients

  • Wheat
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Your Favourite Topping

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degree Farenheit and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the wheat flour and sugar.
  3. Slowly add the milk to the flour. Stir well until the dough comes together
  4. Add your favourite topping when the dough is formed
  5. Take small portion of the dough and roll them into balls and flatten each dough balls
  6. Bake in the preheated over for 10-12 minutes and enjoy
